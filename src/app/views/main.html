Welcome to the TERN Data Portal

Welcome to the Texas Estuarine Resource Network (TERN!) Audubon Texas's Coastal Conservation Program has a continued legacy of protecting and managing rookery islands for colonial waterbird populations since 1923 and TERN volunteers are an integral part of this process.

Our mission of sustaining important bird populations and engaging millions of people of all ages and backgrounds in conservation can only be achieved with you - boots on the ground for healthy bird habitats. If you are interested in listening to what birds have to tell us, and helping to reverse the dangerous trends they indicate in their habitats, you've come to the right place.

Follow the instructions below to get connected, share your data, learn more about citizen science, and join our family of conservationists on the Texas Coast.
